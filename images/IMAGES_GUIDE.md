# Image Replacements Guide

Replace each file below with your HD photo. Recommended size: **1200×800px minimum**, JPG or WebP.

| Filename | Used In | What to photograph |
|---|---|---|
| `kirana-grocery-bags.jpg` | Case Study 1 | Clear LDPE carry bags, stacked or fanned out, ideally with groceries inside |
| `industrial-ldpe-liners.jpg` | Case Study 2 | Heavy-gauge liner bags inside a drum or carton, showing the protective fit |
| `zipper-resealable-pouches.jpg` | Case Study 3 | Close-up of zipper pouches, open and closed, with contents visible |
| `multilayer-laminated-packaging.jpg` | Case Study 4 (Featured) | Laminated film rolls or finished atta/detergent pouches showing layers |
| `custom-printed-solutions.jpg` | Case Study 5 | Before/after: plain roll vs vibrant branded pouch, or printing press in action |

**Tips:**
- Shoot on a clean white or neutral background for product shots
- Include a scale reference where useful (ruler, hand)
- Bright, even lighting — avoid harsh shadows
- High contrast between product and background
