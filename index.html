Case Studies

Products That Solve Real Problems

Every packaging format below was developed in response to specific industry challenges — from shelf-life extension to industrial bulk protection.

Retail

LD Kirana & Grocery Bags

Challenge: Kirana stores needed a carry bag that maintains clarity for product visibility while resisting tearing under load.

Solution: Food-grade LDPE bags at 51+ microns — CPCB compliant, fully recyclable, and tested for tear resistance and puncture strength.

Material LDPE (Food-Grade)
Thickness 51+ Microns (CPCB)
Compliance 100% Recyclable
Food-Grade CPCB Compliant High Clarity
Industrial

Industrial LDPE Liners

Challenge: Chemical drum and bulk carton operators faced moisture ingress and dust contamination during long-distance transit.

Solution: Heavy-gauge LDPE liner bags engineered for drum and carton interiors — creating a sealed moisture and dust barrier throughout the supply chain.

Use Case Chemical Drums, Cartons
Thickness 80–150 Microns
Barrier Moisture & Dust
Heavy-Gauge Moisture Barrier Custom Dimensions
Consumer

Zipper & Resealable Pouches

Challenge: Snack, stationery, and hardware brands needed tamper-evident, resealable packaging that maintains freshness after the first open.

Solution: Precision zipper-inserted pouches with heat-sealed edges — offering a secure, leak-proof reseal for snacks, hardware fasteners, and personal care items.

Seal Type Zip-Lock + Heat Seal
Applications Snacks, Stationary, Hardware
Finish Transparent / Frosted
Resealable Leak-Proof Precision Zipper

Most Popular

Multi-Layer Laminated Packaging

Challenge: Flour (atta), detergent, and dry food brands needed packaging that blocks oxygen and moisture to extend shelf life beyond standard single-layer films.

Solution: 2-layer and 3-layer structures (PET / MET-PET / LDPE) providing exceptional oxygen and moisture barrier properties — dramatically extending product shelf life.

Structure PET / MET-PET / LDPE
Barrier O₂ + Moisture
Applications Atta, Detergent, Dry Foods
3-Layer Structure Oxygen Barrier Extended Shelf Life
Branding

Custom Printed Solutions

Challenge: Consumer brands needed print quality that matches global shelf-ready standards on flexible packaging — fast turnaround, consistent color reproduction.

Solution: Up to 8-color rotogravure and flexographic printing on BOPP, PET, and laminate substrates. Our process delivers photographic-quality branding with consistent color registration.

Print Method Rotogravure / Flexo
Colors Up to 8 Colors
